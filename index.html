BattleStats RPG Tool

Welcome to BattleStats

BattleStats is a tool to build and check the stats for RPG character and NPC builds. With this tool, you can check how different characters, armor, and weapons will affect the tides of battle, and run thousands of iterations to determine how well your creations are balanced.

Currently this tool is under construction, and will be availalbe with limited features soon!
